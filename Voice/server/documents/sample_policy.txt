=== KRETA BANDHU SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY ===
- Free shipping: We offer free standard shipping on all orders above 499 INR. For orders below 499 INR, a flat shipping fee of 40 INR is charged.
- Delivery timelines:
  * Metro cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Kolkata, Chennai, Hyderabad): 1 to 3 business days.
  * Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities: 3 to 5 business days.
  * Other remote locations: 5 to 7 business days.
- Tracking: You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. You will also receive an SMS with a tracking link once your order is shipped.

=== KRETA BANDHU RETURN & REFUND POLICY ===
- Return window: Most items are eligible for return or exchange within 30 days from the delivery date. Items must be unused, in their original packaging, and have tags intact.
- Exceptions: Electronics and home appliances are eligible for a 7-day replacement only if they are received damaged or defective. Personal care items, innerwear, and customized products cannot be returned.
- Refund timeline: Once the returned item reaches our warehouse and passes quality checks, the refund is initiated. It takes 7 to 10 business days for the refund amount to reflect in your original payment method (bank account, credit card, or UPI). For instant refunds, you can choose refund to your Kreta Bandhu Wallet.
