KaliGan AI Company Information Document

Overview:
KaliGan AI is an advanced multi-tenant AI lead-capture platform that integrates chat and voice capabilities. It helps businesses convert visitors into qualified, scored leads automatically.

Founders & Leadership:
The company was founded in 2026 by Parth and Ganesh. The research and engineering headquarters are located in Bangalore, India.
